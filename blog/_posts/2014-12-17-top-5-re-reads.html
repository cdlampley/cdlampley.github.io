I'm doing a Top 5 Wednesday on a Wednesday! But of course I'm not doing the current week's topic. That would be too normal for me. I'm doing last weeks. My top 5 re-reads. I don't re-read books a lot. Not really. I've decided to list the 5 books I want to re-read, maybe next year. Possibly next year. At least a couple of them, I believe I will re-read next year. Here they are.

[gallery size="medium" ids="2690,2689,2688,2693,2692,2694"]

1. The Devil Wears Prada by Lauren Weisberger

I haven't read this in over 10 years. No wait. I think I read it again before the movie came out. Still, it has been a while.

2. Harry Potter Series by J.K. Rowling

This will be re-read always. ALWAYS.

3. Vicious by V.E. Schwab

Oh how I love this book. Oh how I want to know what these characters are doing right now.

4. The History of Love by Nicole Krauss

This book is a beauty. Haven't read it in years and the re-read is overdue.

5. The Girls Guide to Hunting and Fishing by Melissa Bank

This book is definitely getting re-read in 2014. Most likely January. We will see if I remember when I do my TBR for next month.
